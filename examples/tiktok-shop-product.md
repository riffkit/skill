# Example — riff a proven format for your product

**You have:** a product (a physical item or an app screen) and a winning short video in your niche.
**You want:** the same attention-grabbing format, but selling *your* thing.

Tell your agent, in plain language:

```
riff https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/123 into a video for my product, in English
```

What Riffkit does:

1. **Studies the source formula** — the hook, the pacing, the emotional beats that make it work.
2. **Rebuilds it around your product** — placed *into* the story the format calls for, not bolted onto a generic stock avatar.
3. **Returns a post-ready video** — voiceover, on-screen captions timed to the audio, a cover frame, and a caption with hashtags.

The source clip is never re-uploaded — the result is your own original.
